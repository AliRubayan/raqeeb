#  Raqeeb (رقيب)
**Multi-Agent Legal Compliance System & Consulting Chatbot for Saudi Law Firms**

Raqeeb is an AI-powered platform designed to help legal professionals and law firms review contracts in minutes. It provides real-time legal risk detection, regulation-backed justification (SAMA & PDPL), AI-powered clause rewriting, and an interactive consulting chatbot for immediate legal discussions.

## Key Features
* **Multi-Agent Workflow:** Utilizes a collaborative approach with distinct AI agents (Inspector, Legislator, and Drafter) to ensure 100% grounded legal reasoning.
* **Interactive Consulting Chatbot:** A real-time Decision Room where lawyers can chat with the AI to discuss flagged issues, request specific clause modifications, and get instant legal explanations.
* **Real-Time Detection:** Scans contracts and identifies clauses violating Saudi Central Bank (SAMA) or PDPL regulations.
* **Actionable Alternatives:** Doesn't just flag errors; it drafts legally compliant alternative clauses instantly.

##  Technical Architecture
* **Automation & Orchestration:** n8n
* **LLM Engine:** Groq (Llama Models)
* **Frontend & Real-time Chat:** Replit / Stream
